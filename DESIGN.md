# Design System: SF TENNIS KIDS
**Project ID:** 2335926853665945122

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design is **Academic**, **Premium**, and **Prestigious**, reflecting the association with San Francisco State University. It uses a high-contrast palette of deep navy and vibrant orange to create a sense of heritage and energy. The layout is airy and structured, with a focus on typography and clear visual hierarchy.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Deep Navy Primary (#1A237E):** The cornerstone color for trust and authority. Used for navigation, primary buttons, and major headings.
*   **Vibrant Accent Orange (#F57C00):** Symbolizes energy and tennis. Used for iconography, subtle accents, hover states, and call-outs.
*   **Court Clay Red (#D32F2F):** Used for critical alerts, rain delays, and urgent notifications.
*   **Sleek Obsidian (#0A0D2E):** Primary text color for deep contrast on light backgrounds.
*   **Mist Gray Background (#F8F9FA):** Main page background to keep the interface feeling professional and clean.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Headers:** **Playfair Display**. A sophisticated serif that provides an academic and established feel. Used for H1 and major section titles.
*   **Body & UI:** **Inter**. A crisp, modern sans-serif designed for legibility across all screen sizes. Used for navigation, body copy, and status indicators.
*   **Weight Usage:** Heavy (Black/Bold) for main hero titles, Medium for subheaders, and Regular for detailed descriptions.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Navigation:** Sticky backdrop with glass-morphism effects (blur/opacity). Thin borders delineate sections without clutter.
*   **Buttons:** Softly rounded (8px for standard, 12px/xl for hero). Clear shadow depth (xl) for primary actions.
*   **Cards:** Clean, bordered containers with subtle elevation. Icon backgrounds use primary-tinted overlays for coherence.
*   **Interactive Elements:** Transitions for color shifts and subtle scaling on hover to provide feedback.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Grid:** Standard 12-column grid with a maximum width of 1280px (7xl).
*   **Hierarchy:** Large, striking hero sections followed by structured feature grids and dynamic snapshots.
*   **Responsibility:** Mobile-first approach with fluid scaling for tablet and desktop viewports.
